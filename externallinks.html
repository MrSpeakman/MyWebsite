Gigapixel Images:


Link to Andromeda Galaxy:

This 69536 x 22230 (1.5 billion) pixel image was taken by the NASA/ESA Hubble Space Telescope on 05 January 2015, 23:15.
It shows a patch of the Andromeda galaxy approxiately 40,000 light years wide containing over 100 million stars at a distance of 200 million light years away.
Credit: NASA, ESA, J. Dalcanton (University of Washington, USA), B. F. Williams (University of Washington, USA), L. C. Johnson (University of Washington, USA), the PHAT team, and R. Gendler.

Link to Carina Nebula:

This 29566 x 14321 pixel image was taken by NASA/ESA Hubble Space Telescope on 24 April 2007, 15:00.
The image shows a nebula called 'Carina' located approximately 7,500 light years away from Earth, the image is approximately 40,000 light years across
and show a very busy star birthing region that is also full of older giant stars coming to the end of their life.
Credit: NASA, ESA, N. Smith (University of California, Berkeley), and The Hubble Heritage Team (STScI/AURA).




Websites:


NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory:

The NASA JPL (Jet Propulsion Laboratory) photojournal is a collection of every image ever taken by NASA since 1996 to the current day.


Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC):

This is a link to the Lunar Reconnaissance orbiter that has been taking high resolution photos of the Moon since 2009 and has mapped the whole surface. It allows you view any part of the Moon in extremely high detail.


ESA Sky:

ESA Sky is a interactive zoomable explorer with a huge range of feature created by the European Space Agency.


WorldWide Telescope:

WorldWide Telescope is an online interactive universe explorer with a huge range of feature for explore.




Space Games:


Celestia:

Celestia is a free 'real-time' space exploration game.


Digital Universe:

Digital Universe is a very complex space exploration software designed by NASA.


Open Space:

Open Space is a solar system exploration game created by NASA, it allows the user to explore the solar system using real data and images.


Orbiter 2016:

Orbiter 2016 is an extremely complex space flight simulator, using real space craft and exact replicas of heads up display, this game will seriously challenge your flying skills!


Outerra Anteworld:

Outerra Anteworld is an exact 1:1 scale replica of Planet Earth with countless real life features in their correct location.


Space Engine:

Space Engine is free for an older version or can be bought for the latest version, it is a 1:1 scale replica of the entire universe with a catalogie of hundreds of thousands of real objects in our universe.


Stellarium:

Stellarium is a perfct piece of software for any astronomer allowing you to find any object from any set location to find in your telescope.


Winstar:

Winstar is another amazing planetarium for any astronomer to find objects and allow you to explore the solar system in great detail.

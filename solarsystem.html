The Solar System

Images Courtesy: NASA/ESO/JPL/Space Science Institute

The solar system is our local gravitationaly bound area of space consisting of 1 star, 8 planets, 214 (known) moons and trillions of comets and asteroids.
It takes approximately 250 million years to orbit the galaxy, 27,000 light years away from the center.
